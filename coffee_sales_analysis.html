import pandas as pd

# قراءة البيانات
df = pd.read_csv("coffee_sales.csv")
df["Sales"] = df["Quantity"] * df["Unit_Price"]

# أعلى منتج مبيعاً (بالنسبة المئوية)
total_qty = df["Quantity"].sum()
top_product = df.groupby("Product")["Quantity"].sum().sort_values(ascending=False)
top_product_name = top_product.index[0]
top_product_percent = round(top_product.iloc[0] / total_qty * 100, 1)

# أضعف منتج مبيعاً (بالنسبة المئوية)
weak_product_name = top_product.index[-1]
weak_product_percent = round(top_product.iloc[-1] / total_qty * 100, 1)

# أفضل يوم مبيعات
df["Date"] = pd.to_datetime(df["Date"])
sales_by_day = df.groupby(df["Date"].dt.day_name())["Sales"].sum().sort_values(ascending=False)
best_day = sales_by_day.index[0]
best_day_value = round(sales_by_day.iloc[0], 2)

# أفضل متجر
sales_by_store = df.groupby("Store")["Sales"].sum().sort_values(ascending=False)
top_store = sales_by_store.index[0]
top_store_percent = round(sales_by_store.iloc[0] / sales_by_store.sum() * 100, 1)

# طباعة الأرقام
print("Top Product:", top_product_name, "-", top_product_percent, "%")
print("Weak Product:", weak_product_name, "-", weak_product_percent, "%")
print("Best Day:", best_day, "-", "$", best_day_value)
print("Top Store:", top_store, "-", top_store_percent, "% of total revenue")

Top Product: Cappuccino - 20.5 %
Weak Product: Americano - 18.7 %
Best Day: Tuesday - $ 650.5
Top Store: Baghdad-Center - 51.9 % of total revenue

Insights & Recommendations¶

Top Product: Cappuccino accounts for 20.5% of total units sold.

  • Recommendation: Run a "Buy 2 Get 1 Medium" promo to encourage trial and upsell customers to Large sizes.

Weak Product: Americano represents only 18.7% of total orders.

  • Recommendation: Try bundling with a cake/pastry or offering a morning discount to boost demand.

Best Day: Tusday shows the highest sales (total = $650).

  • Recommendation: Focus weekend campaigns and family offers from Thursday evening through Friday.

Store Performance: Baghdad-Center outperforms other branches by 51.9%.

  • Recommendation: Analyze the success factors (location traffic, peak hours, promotions) and replicate them across other stores.
